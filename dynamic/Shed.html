About us

The Test Valley Men’s Shed is a community project in Laverstoke, Hampshire. It provides a space where older gentlemen can gather and work on practical projects of their own choice, in addition to providing a great place to talk. Come and visit to see what we’re up to..

Opening Times and Workshop

Tuesday & Thursday 9:00 - 12:00

The Shed is now open on Tuesday and Thursday mornings between 9 am and 12 pm. Our first project has been to repair the rotten flooring in the building to make it safe. We’ve also opened up the building to provide a huge space which we’ll use as the main workshop. If you have time on your hands and you’d like to get involved in projects at Men’s Shed, send us an email or visit the Shed on Laverstoke lane.

Join us today and become a part of our growing community!

Lottery funded

A huge thank you to all the National Lottery players in the UK. The Test Valley Men’s Shed is now the proud recipient of Lottery Funding. We’re going to use the money to open for at least one further day per week and install a wood-burning stove which will allow us to open during the winter months. Another plan is to hire a skip to clear away rubbish from the building.
